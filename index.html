Swaralipi

ABOUT

Swaralipi is new concept for creating musical composition of Indic genres using Indic music notation systems. The primary objective of this concept is to provide a new mean to transcribe, render, display, store and play Indic music using Indic music notation systems. To make the concept into reality, we have postulated a new framework for transcribing and rendering Indic music sheet. We have also designed and developed a new XML format for storing and exchanging musical information accross web.

Any new contribution will be available in this website. You can view the latest updates by clicking on the tab links.
